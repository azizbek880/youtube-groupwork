Future Plans

Where we plan to go next with this project and channel.

We plan to grow GOLAZOO by:

  • Increasing video production and quality
  • Expanding website features (e.g. more quizzes, polls, and blog content)
  • Reaching 1,000+ subscribers
  • Collaborating with other football content creators
  • Maintaining a consistent upload and promotion schedule

Long-term, we aim to make GOLAZOO a recognized football media brand for youth and aspiring analysts.
